Who We Are

Engineering Excellence
Since 2013

NDNS Engineers is a civil engineering consultancy providing structural and building construction services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Founded in Hubli and operating across India and internationally, we bring over 12 years of field-tested expertise to every project.

We believe in delivering services that are functional, economical, and innovative — with an uncompromising commitment to quality and timely delivery. Our international footprint includes projects in Qatar, Nepal, and the Middle East.

Our office is located at Marvel Artiza, Vidyanagar, Hubli — one of the city's landmark green buildings.
